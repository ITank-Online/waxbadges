About WAXBadges
Achievements logged on the blockchain for eternity! Keep what you earn!
WAXBadges is an open Achievements platform that safely preserves your achievements forever on the WAX blockchain. Even if the game or company disappears, your achievements will always live on!

Any game studio or app developer can easily integrate their achievements into WAXBadges at almost zero cost.

Learn more at https://github.com/kdmukai/waxbadges
